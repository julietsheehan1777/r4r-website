About Research!America

The charity making our mission possible

What is Research!America?

Research!America is a leading nonprofit organization dedicated to making medical, health, and scientific research a higher national priority. They advocate for increased funding for research and work to improve both the American and global healthcare systems through evidence-based policy and innovation.

Founded in 1989, Research!America has become the nation's largest not-for-profit public education and advocacy alliance, uniting members across the research and health sectors with millions of Americans committed to saving lives through research.

Visit Research!America Website

Why Research!America?

Research!America supports:

  • Medical Research Funding: Advocates for sustained investment in biomedical research through the NIH and other agencies
  • Global Health Initiatives: Promotes research that addresses worldwide health challenges
  • Health Innovation: Supports policies that accelerate the development of new treatments and cures
  • Patient Advocacy: Gives voice to patients and families affected by diseases
  • Research Infrastructure: Advocates for the resources researchers need to make breakthroughs

Donation Information

When you participate in Recycle4Research, we ask that you donate to Research!America in exchange for our free glass pickup service. While donations are voluntary, we recommend contributing $120 per year (or about $10 per month).

You can donate directly to Research!America through their website, or contact us for alternative donation methods. All donations are tax-deductible as allowed by law.

Legal Information

Tax-Deductible Donations

Research!America is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Tax ID: 52-1546725

Our Relationship

Recycle4Research is an independent community initiative that supports Research!America through participant donations. We are not directly affiliated with or sponsored by Research!America, but we partner with them to advance medical research funding.

Donation Policy

Donations to Research!America are voluntary but strongly encouraged. Recycle4Research does not handle donations directly - all contributions should be made directly to Research!America through their official channels.
