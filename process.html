Steps to Get Started

1

Sign Up Through Our Form

Fill out our simple registration form to get started. We'll collect your address and contact information to coordinate bin delivery and pickups.

Sign Up Now
2

Pay the $25 Bin Fee

A one-time fee of $25 covers the cost of your recycling bin. This bin is yours to keep and use for all future pickups.

Payment methods: Venmo, Zelle, or Cash

3

Receive Your Bin

We'll drop off your glass recycling bin at your house. The bin will be clearly labeled with recycling criteria.

4

Collect Glass

Fill your bin with used glass that meets our recycling criteria. See below for what we accept.

5

Leave Bin Outside on Pickup Days

On designated pickup days (usually every other week unless directed otherwise), leave the bin outside your house in a visible area for us to collect. We'll empty it and return it to your doorstep.
